A New Home for Jenkins X

Jenkins X is joining the newly created Continuous Delivery Foundation (CD Foundation or CDF).

The Continuous Delivery Foundation (CD Foundation or CDF) serves as the vendor-neutral home of many of the fastest-growing projects for continuous delivery, including Jenkins, Jenkins X, Spinnaker, and Tekton.
